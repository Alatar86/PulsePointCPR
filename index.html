Frequently Asked Questions

How long does CPR certification last?

CPR certification is valid for 2 years from the date of completion. We offer recertification classes to keep your skills current.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We provide special rates for businesses, schools, and groups of 5 or more. Contact us for custom pricing.

What should I bring to class?

Just bring yourself and comfortable clothing. All training materials, equipment, and certification cards are provided.

Are classes available on weekends?

Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends to accommodate your busy schedule.
